This is an example of a work “side-project” that inspired by seeing a persistent pain point for a lot of engineers. This is an internal tool I designed and built with a team member allows you to quickly spin up stage accounts for testing purposes. The existing tool left a lot to be desired, to say the least. This tool created accounts with a single click, and allowed you to save and share complicated account types easily. After a couple of weeks, or so, we were able to share our solution with our organization and got good adoption for the new tool.
