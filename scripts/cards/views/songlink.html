Songlink is a URL creation tool — featured on LifeHacker — that allows people to share music easily. During the development of Songlink I undertook both back-end developer and designer responsibilities. On the back-end I was integral in developing the API that queried third-party catalogs for song information, and on the front-end I designed and implemented each page layout. I was also central in conceptualizing a request system involving server-side page rendering and CDN caching through AWS.
