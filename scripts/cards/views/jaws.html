Our team prioritized the freedom to address general pain points unrelated to our product. Of the various issues I worked on, this tool was one of my favorites. This is an internal tool I designed and built with a team member which allows you to quickly spin up stage accounts for testing purposes.

The existing tool left a lot to be desired, to say the least. This tool, though, created accounts with a single click, and allowed you to save, share, and recreate custom account types easily. It was built with Electron (not the fastest for the CPU :), but it allowed us to build it quickly) so we could add global keyboard shortcuts to create and paste new account emails into staging apps on the fly. After a couple of weeks, or so, we were able to share our solution with our organization and got good adoption for the new tool.
